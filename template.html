As of the 31st of June 2017 the Zabututi Government has introducted a new tax scheme. This will slightly increase the amount that each citizen pays anually through income tax and in Goods and Services tax (GST). For information on the GST changes please visit that section of this website.
These changes will allow the Government to devote more funds to schools, hospitals and roads, enabling them to keep up to date with modern technology and the depands of our growing population. Ensuring Zabututi is a wonderful place to live for many years to come.
This website features an income tax calculator, a helpful tool that will allow you to find out how much you will need to pay anually, with this nrw scheme.
